4. Design Notes

Phase 1 вҖ” Sonic Intent

Three independent groups of 6 first-order all-pass stages cascaded in series per stereo channel вҖ” 18 all-pass stages total per channel. Mixed with the dry signal, they create the classic phaser comb: a sweep of notches and peaks across the frequency spectrum. With three independent groups tuned to different frequency ranges, the comb pattern spreads across the full audio band, producing rich, formant-like resonances вҖ” like a vowel filter that breathes with modulation.

  • Group 1: Low formant (~100 Hz вҖ“ 1 kHz)
  • Group 2: Mid formant (~500 Hz вҖ“ 5 kHz)
  • Group 3: High formant (~2 kHz вҖ“ 20 kHz)

FB DIST BLEND: replaces the former 3-position SOURCE switch (Internal / Blend / Post-Dist). The continuous knob smoothly crossfades the feedback source from clean APF output (0%) to post-distortion signal (100%). All intermediate values are musically useful.

COMB BYPASS: replaces the former DRY/WET mix control. Controls the pre-comb VCA level вҖ” at 0%, the signal bypasses the comb chain entirely; at 100%, full comb processing is applied.

Phase 2 вҖ” Transfer Functions

Single 1st-order APF stage:
  H_APF(s) = (s вҲ’ ПүвӮҖ) / (s + ПүвӮҖ)
  Magnitude: |H_APF(jПү)| = 1 for all Пү (unity gain вҖ” all-pass)
  Phase: ПҶ(Пү) = ПҖ вҲ’ 2 arctan(Пү / ПүвӮҖ)

Six cascaded stages (one group):
  H_6(s) = [(s вҲ’ ПүвӮҖ) / (s + ПүвӮҖ)]^6
  Phase: ПҶ_6(Пү) = 6 Г— [ПҖ вҲ’ 2 arctan(Пү / ПүвӮҖ)]

Notch frequencies:
  Пү_notch_n = ПүвӮҖ Г— tan((5 вҲ’ 2n)ПҖ / 12)   for n = 0, 1, 2  вҶ’ 3 notch pairs per group

OTA voltage control:
  ПүвӮҖ = g_m / C_apf
  g_m = I_abc / (2 Г— V_T)   V_T вүҲ 26 mV at room temp
  1V/oct: +1V вҶ’ I_abc Г— 2 вҶ’ ПүвӮҖ Г— 2 вҶ’ f Г— 2

Capacitor values (using I_abc_nom = 10 ВөA, g_m = 192 ВөS):
  Group 1 (100 HzвҖ“1 kHz,  Пү_max = 6283):   C = g_m/Пү = 30.6 nF вҶ’ 33 nF C0G
  Group 2 (500 HzвҖ“5 kHz,  Пү_max = 31416):  C = g_m/Пү = 6.1 nF  вҶ’ 6.8 nF C0G
  Group 3 (2 kHzвҖ“20 kHz,  Пү_max = 125664): C = g_m/Пү = 1.53 nF вҶ’ 1.5 nF C0G

  Note: earlier versions used I_abc_max = 500 ВөA giving 1000Г— too-small values.
  The correct derivation always uses the nominal operating current (10 ВөA).

Feedback transfer function (g = feedback gain, 0 вүӨ g < 1):
  V_fb_source = (1 вҲ’ FB_DIST_BLEND) Г— V_apf_out + FB_DIST_BLEND Г— V_post_dist
  V_out = H_6(s) Г— V_in / (1 вҲ’ g Г— POLARITY Г— H_6(s))
  At g вҶ’ 0.95: resonance approaching self-oscillation at notch frequencies

Phase 3 вҖ” Circuit Notes

  • Expo converter matching: THAT340 matched NPN pair required for stable 1V/oct tracking. LM394 is discontinued вҖ” use THAT340 only. Calibrate at room temperature; verify at temperature extremes.
  • HF oscillation risk: at high I_abc (high frequency), OTA stages can self-oscillate due to parasitic capacitance. 22 pF C0G caps are now in the parts list (C_hf_x) вҖ” place at each OTA output node. Keep OTA output traces short.
  • Linearizing resistors: 1 kО© resistors at each LM13700 OTA differential input (IN+ and INвҲ’) are in the parts list (R_lin_x). These are required for low distortion at signal levels above Вұ26 mV at the OTA inputs.
  • FB DIST BLEND circuit: continuous complementary resistive crossfade + op-amp buffer. No CD4053 needed. Buffer the BLEND_AMP output before the per-group feedback summing amps to isolate the crossfade pot loading from downstream.
  • POLARITY Off position: must connect cleanly to GND вҖ” a leaky switch contact causes audible bleed-through. Use a low-leakage panel switch or add a bleeder resistor to GND at the switch output.
  • V_post_dist routing: distortion output (Block 4) must be routed to APF feedback without picking up noise. Use shielded wire or dedicated PCB trace with guard ring; keep away from OTA signal nodes.
  • Crosstalk L/R: route L and R ground planes separately; do not route L signal adjacent to R signal on PCB.
  • PCB count: 18Г— LM13700 + 18Г— TL072 is large. Plan for 2вҖ“3 stacked PCBs with ribbon cable interconnect.
