4. Design Notes

Phase 1 вҖ” Sonic Intent

Three independent distortion instances вҖ” one per APF group output вҖ” each with its own mode and drive level. Each instance processes the stereo (L+R) output of its APF chain equally. The three distorted group signals are summed after processing and passed to LP1.

This per-chain arrangement means each formant band can be distorted differently: Group 1 (low) wavefolded for complex sub-harmonic content, Group 2 (mid) soft-clipped for warmth, Group 3 (high) hard-clipped for bright edge вҖ” or any combination.

  • Soft Clip: Gentle, musical saturation. Warm odd harmonics. Sweet spot: 30вҖ“60%.
  • Hard Clip: Aggressive transistor-style clipping, strong odd harmonics. Sweet spot: 20вҖ“50%.
  • Wavefold: Buchla-style wavefolding. Complex harmonics increase with drive. Sweet spot: 40вҖ“75%.

Phase 2 вҖ” Transfer Functions

Two-Zone Drive Mapping (all modes):

  Zone 1 вҖ” Volume (param вүӨ 0.20):
    out = in Г— (param / 0.20)
    Linear attenuation from mute (0.0) to unity (0.20). No distortion; all three modes identical.

  Zone 2 вҖ” Distortion (param > 0.20):
    d = (param вҲ’ 0.20) / 0.80      вҶҗ normalized drive factor, 0 вҶ’ 1
    Apply per-mode transfer function below with drive factor d.
    Unity gain at d = 0; full drive at d = 1.

  CV note: 0вҖ“10 V range; 0 V = mute, 2 V = unity (9am), 10 V = full drive.
  Hardware: gain = R_f / R_in; unity when R_in = R_f = 10kО© (20% of 50kО© linear pot).
  R_min_series вүҲ 470О© (prevents вҲһ gain at full CW); max gain вүҲ 21Г— (full drive).

Soft Clip:
  y = tanh(d Г— x) / tanh(d)
  where x = normalized input (вҲ’1 to +1 = Вұ5 V full scale)
        d = drive factor from two-zone mapping above (0 вҶ’ 1 вҶ’ beyond)
  At d вҶ’ 0: y вүҲ x (linear)
  At d вҶ’ вҲһ: y = sign(x) (approaches hard clip)
  Even harmonics: none (symmetrical clipping вҶ’ odd harmonics only)

Hard Clip:
  y = clamp(d Г— x, вҲ’1, +1)
  d = 1: clip onset at Вұfull scale
  d = 5: clip at Вұ1 V input вҖ” strong rectangular waveform content
  Strong odd harmonics; abrupt transition (unlike soft clip)

Wavefold:
  y = fold(d Г— x)
  fold(z): if |z| вүӨ 1: y = z
           if 1 < |z| вүӨ 3: y = 2 вҲ’ z (positive z)
           if 3 < |z| вүӨ 5: y = вҲ’4 + z (positive z)  [repeating]
  d = 1: no folding
  d = 2: single fold
  d = 4вҖ“5: multiple folds, complex spectral content
  Mix of even and odd harmonics depending on DRIVE setting.

Phase 3 вҖ” Circuit Notes

  • Summing headroom: three chains summed at high drive can exceed op-amp rail. Gain = 0.5Г— per chain (R_f = R_sum/2) keeps worst-case combined output to Вұ7.5 V.
  • CD4053 V_EE = вҲ’12 V (CRITICAL): The CD4053 must have V_EE connected to вҲ’12 V, V_DD to +12 V, V_SS to GND. If V_EE is left at GND, the mux silently distorts all negative-going audio. Label this explicitly on the schematic for all three MUX ICs.
  • CD4053 series resistance: ~100 О© at Вұ5 V supply. Buffer each CD4053 output with a unity-gain op-amp half before the summing stage.
  • Post-Dist feedback routing: each chain's output (before the summing amp) is tapped and routed to Block 3's FB DIST BLEND crossfade circuit for that group. Route as shielded traces to avoid inter-chain crosstalk.
  • MODE switching live: brief click possible from mechanical switching. Accepted behavior.
  • Diode/zener matching: use components from the same batch within each chain. Cross-chain matching less critical.
