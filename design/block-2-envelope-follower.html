4. Design Notes

Phase 1 вҖ” Sonic Intent

Derives CV envelopes from the post-gain / pre-comb audio signal. Two independent followers вҖ” one per channel вҖ” each producing a 0вҖ“10 V CV that tracks the amplitude of the audio in real time. These envelopes are the primary modulation source for the entire module, creating filter sweeps, distortion changes, and formant shifts that are tightly coupled to the dynamics of the input.

  • Fast attack / fast release: percussive, tight CV that punches with every transient.
  • Slow attack / slow release: smooth, sustained CV that swells with the overall level.

Phase 2 вҖ” Analog Behavior Model

Stage 1: Full-wave precision rectifier
  V_rect = |V_in|

Stage 2: Peak detector with asymmetric RC
  Rising edge  (V_rect > V_peak): П„_attack  = R_atk Г— C_hold
  Falling edge (V_rect < V_peak): П„_release = R_rel Г— C_hold

  C_hold = 2.2 ВөF (film)
  Attack range:  П„ = 0.1 ms (pot minimum) вҶ’ 200 ms (pot maximum)
    R_atk_min вүҲ 100 О© (end-stop)  вҶ’ П„ = 100О© Г— 2.2ВөF вүҲ 0.22 ms
    R_atk_max вүҲ 100 kО© (pot + end) вҶ’ П„ = 100kО© Г— 2.2ВөF вүҲ 220 ms
  Release range: П„ = 5 ms вҶ’ 2 s
    R_rel_min вүҲ 2.4 kО© (end-stop)  вҶ’ П„ = 2.4kО© Г— 2.2ВөF вүҲ 5.3 ms
    R_rel_max вүҲ 1 MО© (pot + end)   вҶ’ П„ = 1MО© Г— 2.2ВөF вүҲ 2.2 s

Stage 3: Output scaling amplifier
  V_env = 2 Г— V_peak   (G = 2 non-inverting)
  Full-scale input Вұ5 V вҶ’ V_peak = 5 V вҶ’ V_env = 10 V
  Output clamped to 0вҖ“10 V via 10 V Zener

Phase 3 вҖ” Circuit Notes

  • Film capacitor for C_hold: ceramic caps have high dielectric absorption causing slow voltage droop; use polyester or polypropylene film (through-hole, 5mm or 7.5mm pitch).
  • Log-taper pots: standard log pots concentrate most of the rotation range at the bottom decade. If attack feel is too cramped at slow times, use a linear pot with an RC shaping network for pseudo-log response.
  • Diode leakage: at room temperature, 1N4148 leakage is negligible (~1вҖ“5 nA). At slow release settings with 2.2 ВөF, hold time is set by diode leakage. Acceptable for release times вүӨ 2 s.
  • DC blocking (optional): if signal chain passes significant DC offset, populate a 10 ВөF series cap before the rectifier. Leave footprint unpopulated and bridged by default.
  • MOD SOURCE SEL normalling: the selected ENV signal arrives at the sleeve switching lug of the PRIMARY MOD SOURCE jack (tip-switching Thonkiconn). When a cable is inserted, the tip disconnects the normalled ENV signal.
